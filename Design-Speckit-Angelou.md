# Typography

> Typography expresses hierarchy and brand presence. Angelou's text sizes and styles were developed to balance content density and reading comfort under typical usage conditions.

---

## Typefaces

| Typeface | Weights Used | Role |
|----------|-------------|------|
| Circular | Bold, Book | Primary — headings (H4–H6), body text, labels, caps |
| Noto Serif | Bold | Display — headings H1–H3 (interchangeable with Circular Bold) |

# Grids & Breakpoints

## Mini Unit

The base unit for the grid system is **8px**. All spacing, sizing, and layout values are multiples of 8.

---

## Breakpoints

| Name | Range |
|------|-------|
| Infinity | 1441px+ |
| Large | 1025 – 1440px |
| Medium | 769 – 1024px |
| Small | 569 – 768px |
| X-small | 0 – 568px |

---

## Global Site Grid

Used for full-width page layouts.

| Breakpoint | Columns | Margins | Gutters |
|------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Infinity (1441+) | 12 | 40px | 24px |
| Large (1025–1440) | 12 | 40px | 24px |
| Medium (769–1024) | 12 | 40px | 24px |
| Small (569–768) | 8 | 40px | 24px |
| X-small (0–568) | 4 | 16px | 16px |

---

## Content Grids

Used for content areas with increased horizontal margin to improve readability at wide viewports.

| Breakpoint | Columns | Margins | Gutters |
|------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Infinity (1441+) | 12 | 120px | 24px |
| Large (1025–1440) | 12 | 80px | 24px |
| Medium (769–1024) | 12 | 40px | 24px |
| Small (569–768) | 8 | 40px | 24px |
| X-small (0–568) | 4 | 16px | 16px |

> Max width for site content is **1680px**. This is based on a 1920 Full HD display.

---

## Side Panels

Used for left/right panel overlays. Panels only apply on Medium and above — not used on Small or X-small.

| Breakpoint | Columns | Left Margin | Right Margin | Gutters |
|------------|---------|-------------|--------------|---------|
| Infinity (1441+) | 4 | 40px | 24px | 24px |
| Large (1025–1440) | 4 | 40px | 24px | 24px |
| Medium (769–1024) | 4 | 40px | 24px | 24px |
| Small (569–768) | — | — | — | — |
| X-small (0–568) | — | — | — | — |

> There is no min or max width for panels.

---
